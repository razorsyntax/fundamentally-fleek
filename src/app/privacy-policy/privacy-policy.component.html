Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 02/27/2025

Introduction

At Fundamentally, privacy is at the core of our mission. We are dedicated to protecting your personal information and ensuring it is handled with care. We collect only the minimum data necessary for login and user management—specifically your name and email—and we are actively exploring ways to obfuscate this data, even from ourselves, to further enhance your privacy. We do not collect data that identifies you beyond this, and we never sell your information.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Your name and email address, required solely for login and user management. We plan to minimize or obfuscate this data to strengthen your privacy.
  • Anonymous Usage Data: We gather anonymous data to understand how our platform is used. This does not identify you personally.
  • LocalStorage: We use LocalStorage on your device to save login tokens for authentication. This is not used for tracking purposes.

We do not use cookies or any tracking technology.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • Login and User Management: Your name and email enable us to manage your account and provide access to the platform.
  • Understanding Platform Usage: Anonymous usage data helps us improve the platform and enhance your experience.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your data to third parties, nor do we share your personal information with any third parties, except as required by law.

Data Security

Your information is protected on secure servers with appropriate technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users as soon as possible and provide updates as we learn more about the breach.

Our servers are located in the United States. If you access our platform from outside the U.S., your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

Your Rights

You have the following rights over your data:

  • Access: You can request to see the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request updates to your personal information if it’s inaccurate.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information at any time.
  • Subscription Cancellation: You can cancel your subscription whenever you choose.

To exercise these rights, email us at fundamentally.xyz@proton.me.

Data Retention

We keep your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. If you request deletion, we will remove your data from our systems promptly.

Children's Privacy

Our platform is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If we discover we’ve collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Accessibility

We aim to make our platform accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. As a startup, we may not implement accessibility features as quickly as we’d like, but we’re committed to improving and welcome your feedback.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted here, and the “Last Updated” date will reflect the latest revision. We encourage you to check this page periodically.

Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out to us at fundamentally.xyz@proton.me.
