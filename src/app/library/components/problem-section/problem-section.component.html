Problem & Solution

The Current State of Investment Research and Portfolio Management

Investment research and portfolio management is a complex and time-consuming process. Current tools are fragmented, leading to data clutter, wasted time, and poor investment decisions.

Introducing FUNDAMENTALLY

Fundamentally streamlines the investment process with a centralized hub for research, data management, and analysis. Our platform empowers investors to make informed decisions through fundamental analysis, with a customizable evaluation framework, quantifiable scoring, and portfolio management tools.

An easy to use dashboard with actionable indicators and a starting point for your research.

Information should be easy to understand at a glance. FUNDAMENTALLY puts emphasis on the dashboard to be more than merely useful- we strive for meaningful.

All your research about an asset in one manageable place.

FUNDAMENTALLY gives everything you need to save your research to one convenient place: a text editor, document uploader, image gallery, and more.

Custom Evaluations

Create completely custom evaluations with FUNDAMENTALLY's Analysis Builder and use it to create quantifiable scoring to use in your investment decisions.

Your evaluations at a glance!

Once you've created and scored your assets you can immediately perform apples-to-apples comparisons as well as set custom suggested bet sizes to give you the control over how you invest.

Quantifiable Scoring

Research takes time. You don't always have all the information you need to evaluate an asset. With FUNDAMENTALLY you can save a draft of your evaluation and come back at any time.

Position your bets!

A very unique feature is the Bet-Size Manager which gives you an easy way to set your risk tolerance based on the asset's score. It takes the guess work out of figuring out how much you should allocate to an asset.

Easy Technical Analysis

Perform Technical Analysis on the asset or on the entire portfolio as an index using TradingView's advanced charts.
